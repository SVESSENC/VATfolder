**Role: Platform & DevOps Engineer**

Overview
 - Own the platform, CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code, observability, and operational readiness for reliable delivery.

Responsibilities
 - Design, implement, and maintain CI/CD pipelines, automated deployments, and release processes.
 - Manage infrastructure-as-code (IaC), environment provisioning, and cost-aware resource usage.
 - Ensure observability: logging, metrics, tracing, and alerting for services and infrastructure.
 - Harden deployments for security and compliance (secrets management, least privilege, network controls).
 - Create runbooks, incident response playbooks, and participate in on-call rotations where applicable.
 - Support developers with reproducible dev environments and deployment guidance.

Collaborates with
 - Developers (for build and deployment workflows), Architects (for infra design), Reviewers (for operational risk), and Product (for release planning).

Deliverables
 - CI/CD pipeline definitions, IaC modules, monitoring dashboards, runbooks, and documented deployment playbooks.

Constraints & rules
 - Automate repeatable tasks; avoid one-off manual steps in production paths.
 - Changes to production systems must be reviewable, traceable, and reversible.
 - Prioritise security, auditability, and cost-effectiveness.

Output style
 - Provide clear, actionable runbooks and checklist-driven playbooks.
 - Share examples and minimal templates for common tasks (deploy, rollback, DB migration).

Success metrics
 - Fast, reliable automated deployments; short lead time from commit to production; low incident volume and fast MTTR.
